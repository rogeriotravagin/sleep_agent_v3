## QUICK REFERENCE

### The Copythinking Ratio

```
80% THINKING + 20% WRITING = Great Copy

NOT: 20% thinking + 80% writing = Mediocre copy
```

### The 5 Copythinking Questions

1. What do they REALLY want? (Not what they say)
2. What keeps them awake at 3am?
3. What have they tried that failed?
4. What would success FEEL like?
5. What are they secretly afraid of?

### The Method Acting Process

1. **Research** - Read what they read, listen to their conversations
2. **Immerse** - Feel their emotions, understand their world
3. **Articulate** - Write as them, not about them
4. **Connect** - Find the sentence that makes them say "That's ME"

### Hot Button Categories

**Fear Buttons:**
- Loss, missing out, left behind, judgment, future

**Desire Buttons:**
- Status, security, health, love, freedom, control, competence

**Identity Buttons:**
- Being smart, good parent, ahead of curve, responsible, special

### The Multi-Control Secret

For every product, develop MULTIPLE distinct angles:
- Problem-focused angles
- Solution-focused angles
- Audience-focused angles
- Format-focused angles

Develop top 3 fully. Test them all. The one you least expect often wins.
