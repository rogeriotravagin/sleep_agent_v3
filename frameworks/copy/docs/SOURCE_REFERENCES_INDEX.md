## Citações por Task

### VSL Script (`expansion-packs/copy/tasks/vsl-script.md`)

#### Frameworks do Inventor Extraídos

**5-Step VSL Process™ Original (2011):**

| Passo | Nome | Timing | Propósito |
|-------|------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | Snap Suggestion Opening | 0-30 segundos | Pattern interrupt que captura atenção |
| 2 | Problem Amplification | 30s - 3 min | Aprofundar dor com empatia |
| 3 | Reluctant Hero Story | 3-8 minutos | Rapport através de vulnerabilidade |
| 4 | Solution Preview | 8-12 minutos | Mostrar possibilidades |
| 5 | Offer & Ethical Close | 12-20 minutos | Oferta irresistível com urgência ética |

Fonte: `outputs/minds/jon_benson/sources/sources_master.yaml`

**3X Formula (2013) - Estrutura de 3 Atos:**

| Ato | Nome | Duração | Citação Chave |
|-----|------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | The Hook | Primeiro 20% | "Hook them within 10 seconds or lose them forever" |
| 2 | The Story | Meio 60% | "The story IS the sale - don't rush it" |
| 3 | The Close | Final 20% | "Make the decision easy and the action clear" |

Fonte: `outputs/minds/jon_benson/sources/sources_master.yaml` - source_002

**5 Tipos de Snap Suggestion:**

| Tipo | Padrão | Mecanismo |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Contradiction | "What if everything you know about [topic] is wrong?" | Desafia crença |
| Impossible Promise | "How I [result] by [unexpected method]" | Bom demais (mas verdade) |
| Taboo Reveal | "The dirty secret [industry] doesn't want you to know" | Conhecimento proibido |
| Pattern Error | "I'm about to tell you NOT to buy my product" | Statement inesperado |
| Time Compression | "In the next 7 minutes, you'll discover..." | Compromisso temporal |

Fonte: `outputs/minds/jon_benson/docs/logs/viability/jon_benson.md`

**5 Beats do Reluctant Hero:**

1. **The Pit** - Mostrar seu ponto mais baixo
2. **The Struggle** - Mostrar tentativas falhas
3. **The Accidental Discovery** - Turning point não planejado
4. **The Transformation** - Mudança gradual e crível
5. **The Mission** - Por que você está compartilhando

Fonte: `outputs/minds/jon_benson/history.yaml`

**5 Tipos de Curiosity Loop:**

1. The Mystery: "In a moment, I'll reveal [thing], but first..."
2. The Tease: "There's one thing that made all the difference..."
3. The Warning: "But before you try this, you MUST know..."
4. The Contrast: "This is nothing like [what they've tried]..."
5. The Insider: "What the [industry] doesn't want you to know..."

Fonte: `outputs/minds/jon_benson` - VSL Psychology framework

**6 Regras de Ethical Persuasion:**

1. Never promise what you can't deliver
2. Use real scarcity, never fake urgency
3. Share authentic stories, not fabricated ones
4. Amplify pain to help, not to manipulate
5. Connect with values, don't exploit fears
6. The goal is to SERVE, not just to sell

Fonte: `outputs/minds/jon_benson` - core philosophy
