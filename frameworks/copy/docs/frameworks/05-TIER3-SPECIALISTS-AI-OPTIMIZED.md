### Benson 5-Step VSL Formula

```yaml
vsl_formula:
  step_1_snap_suggestion:
    timing: "0-15 seconds"
    purpose: "Strong pattern interrupt"
    elements:
      - "Specific promise"
      - "Immediate relevance"
    template: "In the next 12 minutes, I'll reveal..."
  
  step_2_usp_mention:
    timing: "15-60 seconds"
    purpose: "Why this video is different"
    elements:
      - "What you'll discover"
      - "Why keep watching"
    template: "This is different from anything you've ever seen..."
  
  step_3_story:
    timing: "1-5 minutes"
    purpose: "Create connection and credibility"
    elements:
      - "Origin story"
      - "Problem you faced"
      - "Discovery that changed everything"
      - "Transition to teaching"
  
  step_4_education:
    timing: "5-15 minutes"
    purpose: "Deliver real value"
    elements:
      - "Teach core concept"
      - "Prove expertise"
      - "Prepare for pitch"
    note: "Content must be genuinely valuable"
  
  step_5_pitch:
    timing: "15-30 minutes"
    purpose: "Convert viewer to buyer"
    elements:
      - "Smooth transition to offer"
      - "Value stack"
      - "Guarantee"
      - "Urgent CTA"
```

### Benson VSL Production Rules

```yaml
production_rules:
  slide_rule:
    statement: "ONE SLIDE = ONE IDEA"
    detail: "Never more than one sentence per slide"
  
  sentence_fragments:
    statement: "Complete sentences not required"
    detail: "Fragments create rhythm"
  
  timing:
    speed: "1-2 seconds per slide (fast)"
    variation: "Vary speed for emphasis"
  
  viewer_control:
    statement: "NO viewer control"
    detail: "Don't allow skip/accelerate"
    reason: "Creates commitment"

use_cases:
  - "Direct sale VSLs"
  - "Video ads for paid traffic"
  - "YouTube Ads"
  - "Webinar pitches"
  - "Video launches"
```

### Chaperon Soap Opera Sequence

```yaml
soap_opera_sequence:
  concept:
    description: "Emails that tell story in parts"
    mechanism: "Each email opens a 'loop' (curiosity)"
    effect: "Next email closes loop and opens another"
    result: "Reader NEEDS to open next email"
    analogy: "Like Lost, Breaking Bad — addictive series"
  
  psychological_basis:
    name: "Zeigarnik Effect"
    definition: "Mind cannot forget incomplete tasks"
    application: "Open loop creates 'mental itch'"
    resolution: "Only way to resolve: read next email"
```

### Chaperon SOS Structure (5 Emails)

```yaml
sos_structure:
  email_1_set_the_stage:
    purpose: "Introduction and promise"
    elements:
      - "Personal introduction"
      - "Promise of what's coming"
      - "Open first loop"
    closing: "Tomorrow I'll tell you how..."
  
  email_2_high_drama:
    purpose: "Develop story and tension"
    elements:
      - "Develop story"
      - "Create identification"
      - "Increase tension"
    action: "Close loop 1, open loop 2"
  
  email_3_epiphany:
    purpose: "The turning point"
    elements:
      - "Discovery moment"
      - "The 'turn' in the story"
      - "Connect to product"
    action: "Close loop 2, open loop 3"
  
  email_4_hidden_benefits:
    purpose: "Reveal unexpected value"
    elements:
      - "Reveal unexpected benefits"
      - "Social proof/cases"
      - "Build desire"
    action: "Close loop 3, open loop 4"
  
  email_5_urgency_scarcity:
    purpose: "Drive action"
    elements:
      - "Create reason to act now"
      - "Complete offer"
      - "Strong CTA"
    action: "Close all loops"
```

### Ogilvy Principles

```yaml
principles:
  facts_not_adjectives:
    weak: "The best car in the world"
    strong: "11 of the 13 Rolls-Royces produced in 1964 are still running today"
  
  long_copy_sells:
    rules:
      - "Long headlines sell more"
      - "Long body copy sells more"
      - "Readers who read more are more interested"
  
  the_big_idea:
    requirements:
      - "Without Big Idea, campaign goes unnoticed"
      - "Big Idea should last 30 years"
      - "Must be describable in 1 sentence"
  
  research_before_writing:
    rules:
      - "Know product deeply"
      - "Know competition"
      - "Know consumer"
      - "Weeks of research for one headline"
  
  creativity_serves_sales:
    statement: "If it doesn't sell, it isn't creative"
    corollary: "Creativity awards don't pay bills"
```
