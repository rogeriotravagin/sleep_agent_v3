```yaml

      - "Who exactly is your ideal customer?"

      - "What's the Big Promise?"
```

### Todd Brown Two Mechanisms

```yaml
two_mechanisms:
  problem_mechanism:
    definition: "Why the problem exists and why previous solutions failed"
    template: "The reason [common approach] doesn't work is because [hidden cause]..."
    goal: "Make them think 'THAT'S why nothing worked!'"
```

### Georgi RMBC Method

```yaml
rmbc_method:
  overview: "Research → Mechanism → Brief → Copy"
  principle: "Never write from blank page"

  r_research:
    time_allocation: "40% of total project time"
    
    what_to_research:
      - "Exact language market uses"
      - "Specific pains (not generic)"
      - "Solutions already tried"
      - "Why those failed"
      - "Secret desires"
      - "Objections they'll have"
    
    where_to_research:
      - "Amazon reviews of competitor products"
      - "Forums and niche groups"
      - "YouTube comments"
      - "Social media posts"
      - "Customer interviews"
    
    output: "Research document with direct market quotes"

  m_mechanism:
    two_parts:
      problem_mechanism:
        - "Why does the problem exist?"
        - "Why did previous solutions fail?"
        - "What's the 'missing piece'?"
      
      solution_mechanism:
        - "How does your product solve differently?"
        - "What's the unique process/method?"
        - "Why does it work when others don't?"
    
    output: "Clear explanation of why they failed before + why they'll succeed now"

  b_brief:
    instruction: "Answer in COPY FORMAT (not bullet points)"
    
    questions_to_answer:
      - "What's the product origin story?"
      - "Who created it and why?"
      - "How was it discovered/developed?"
      - "What are the main components?"
      - "What are benefits of each component?"
      - "What proof exists?"
      - "What's the complete offer?"
      - "What are the bonuses?"
      - "What's the guarantee?"
      - "What's the CTA?"
    
    output: "Multiple pre-written copy chunks"

  c_copy:
    approach: "ASSEMBLE from brief, not write from scratch"
    
    uses:
      - "Research as language source"
      - "Mechanism as central argument"
      - "Brief as pre-written blocks"
    
    standard_structure:
      I: "Lead (intro with hook)"
      II: "Background story"
      III: "Unique mechanism of problem"
      IV: "Unique mechanism of solution"
      V: "Product build-up and reveal"
      VI: "Offer + stack + guarantee + CTA"
    
    output: "Complete sales letter"

use_cases:
  - "Any structured copy project"
  - "When speed + quality both needed"
  - "Training other copywriters"
  - "Creating production systems"
  - "Scaling copy output"
```
