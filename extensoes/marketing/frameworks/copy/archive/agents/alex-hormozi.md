2. **Perceived Likelihood:** They believe it works
3. **Time Delay:** Fast results
4. **Effort Required:** Little effort
5. **Guarantee:** Removes all risk
6. **Bonuses:** "Free" additional value
7. **Scarcity:** Reason to act now
8. **Price Anchoring:** Shows value much greater than price
